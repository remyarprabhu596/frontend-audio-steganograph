ABOUT

Audio steganography is a technique that involves hiding information within audio files, making it difficult for anyone to detect the hidden data. It can be used for various purposes, including secure communication, data protection, and watermarking.

In audio steganography, information can be concealed within the audio file by subtly altering the audio data. This hidden information is embedded in such a way that it's imperceptible to the human ear and standard audio analysis tools.
